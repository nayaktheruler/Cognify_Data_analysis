# Cognify_Data_analysis Internship
This repository contains the tasks completed during my Data Analysis Internship at Cognifyz Technologies. The project involves exploring, analyzing, and visualizing a restaurant dataset to uncover insights into culinary trends, pricing, and customer preferences.

🚀 Project Overview
The main goal of this internship was to perform a comprehensive analysis of restaurant data across three distinct levels, ranging from basic data exploration to advanced predictive modeling and geospatial analysis.

📂 Internship Levels & Tasks
Level 1: Basic Data Analysis
Task 1: Top Cuisines – Identified the top three most common cuisines and calculated the percentage of restaurants serving them.

Task 2: City Analysis – Determined the city with the highest number of restaurants and compared average ratings across different cities.

Task 3: Price Range Distribution – Visualized the distribution of restaurant price ranges using bar charts and histograms.

Task 4: Online Delivery – Analyzed the percentage of restaurants offering online delivery and compared their ratings with those that don't.

Level 2: Advanced Data Analysis
Task 1: Cuisine Combination – Identified the most common cuisine pairings and checked if specific combinations lead to higher ratings.

Task 2: Geographic Analysis – Used latitude and longitude data to map restaurant locations and identify regional clusters.

Task 3: Restaurant Chains – Identified major restaurant chains and analyzed their performance (ratings vs. popularity).

Level 3: Predictive Modeling & Insights
Task 1: Restaurant Rating Prediction – Built regression models (Linear Regression, Random Forest) to predict restaurant aggregate ratings.

Task 2: Votes Analysis – Investigated the correlation between the number of votes and the overall rating.

Task 3: Price Range vs. Services – Analyzed if higher-priced restaurants are more likely to offer table bookings or online delivery.

🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
Language: Python

Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-Learn, Folium

Environment: Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab

Dataset: Restaurant Dataset (CSV)

📊 Key Insights
Cuisine Trends: North Indian and Chinese remain the most popular cuisines in the dataset.

Delivery Impact: Restaurants offering online delivery generally maintain a higher volume of reviews and slightly better engagement.

Pricing: Most restaurants fall within the low-to-mid price range, with high-end restaurants showing a stronger correlation with table booking services.
